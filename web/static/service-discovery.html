Service Discovery

Configure automatic upstream server discovery from service registries

What is Service Discovery?

Service Discovery automatically finds and configures upstream servers from service registries like Consul, etcd, Eureka, or Kubernetes. It periodically queries the registry and updates your upstream pool.
